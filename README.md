# Vroom

Vroom, Inc. is a Houston-based automotive technology company that operated as an online used vehicle retailer. In January 2024, Vroom ceased direct-to-consumer e-commerce vehicle sales and pivoted to its two B2B subsidiaries: United Auto Credit Corporation (UACC) and CarStory. The company exited Chapter 11 bankruptcy in January 2025.

**UACC** is a non-prime automotive finance lender serving 7,300+ dealerships with digital application origination, instant credit decisions, and 50-state contract printing.

**CarStory** is an AI-powered analytics platform processing 7M+ vehicle listings per day, offering appraisal, promotion, and profit-tracking tools to automotive dealers.
